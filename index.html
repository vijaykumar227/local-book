FAQs

How do bookings work?

Choose a product, select pickup or delivery, and confirm the reservation. You’ll get a confirmation instantly.

Which payment methods are supported?

We support major payment options. You’ll see available methods during checkout.

Is Local Book free for customers?

Browsing is free. Stores may set pricing and fees for certain services.

Do you operate in all cities?

We’re remote-first and expanding continuously. Availability depends on participating stores.
